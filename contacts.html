Colosseum and Vatican


Our tour guides will offer an exciting and memorable tour through the Vatican and Colosseum. Just remember that both of these momuments are the most important in Rome so never forget to bring along your camera for photos which you will for sure take many!


Map of Rome's City Center

This is a brief map of Rome, centering the Colosseum just to show you exactly where it is situated. As you can see it is right in the city center and nearby there is a vast and beautiful park. We have also emphasized in the map, that nearby there are places where you may rent a bike as we have stated previously. Not only, but there are several bars, restaurants b&b's, etc. absolutely everything you may need when visiting downtown Rome.

We have available all year long, special tours for both the Vatican and Colosseum. We also may arrange tours for tourists with special needs such as certain hanicaps, etc. we can deal with anything just as long as tourists let us know prior to arrival in Rome. Airport pick-ups are available and included with our tours so your trip to Rome may be as comfortable as possible during your stay.
